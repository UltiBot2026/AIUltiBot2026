## PRICE NOTES & DISCLAIMERS

**Factors Affecting Final Quote:**
- Roof type and condition (GI sheet vs. concrete)
- Distance from main electrical panel
- Accessibility and installation difficulty
- Specific inverter brand selection (Deye, Solis, GoodWe, SRNE, Sigenergy)
- Current market prices and supplier availability
- Installation location (Metro Manila vs. provincial areas)
- Customization and additional features

**Included in Quotes:**
- High-quality Talesun bifacial solar panels
- Premium inverters (Deye, Solis, or equivalent)
- SoEasy mounting system
- Chint AC/DC breakers and protection devices
- Professional installation and labor
- System design and engineering
- Warranty documentation

**Not Included:**
- Electrical rewiring beyond solar system
- Roof repairs or reinforcement
- Permit and inspection fees
- Grid connection application fees (varies by location)
- Maintenance after warranty period

**Estimated Price Trends (2026-2030):**
- Solar panel costs expected to decrease 3-5% annually
- Inverter prices relatively stable with minor fluctuations
- Installation costs may increase due to labor inflation
- Overall system cost per kW expected to decrease 2-4% annually
- Hybrid systems with batteries may see 5-8% annual cost reduction

**Warranty Coverage:**
- Solar Panels: 10 years (Talesun)
- Inverters: 5-10 years (brand dependent)
- Installation: 2 years workmanship
- Battery (if included): 5-10 years
