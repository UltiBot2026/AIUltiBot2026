## Step 1: Access Automations

### Method A: Via Facebook Page (Recommended)

1. **Go to your Facebook Page**
   - Open Facebook in your browser
   - Navigate to your "Ultiphoton Solar Power OPC" page
   - Make sure you're logged in as a page admin

2. **Access Page Settings**
   - Click the **"Settings"** button (usually at the top right of your page)
   - Look for a gear icon or "Settings" option

3. **Find Automations**
   - In the left menu, scroll down and look for **"Messaging"**
   - Click on **"Messaging"**
   - You should see options like:
     - Instant Replies
     - Frequently Asked Questions
     - Greetings

4. **Alternative Path:**
   - If you don't see it, try: Settings → Inbox → Automations
   - Or go to: Settings → Messaging → Automations

## Step 2: Set Up Instant Reply (Welcome Message)

### What is Instant Reply?
This is the automatic message people see when they message your page for the first time.

### Setup Instructions:

1. **Enable Instant Replies**
   - In Messaging settings, find **"Instant Replies"**
   - Toggle the switch to **ON** (it should turn blue)

2. **Add Your Welcome Message**
   - Click in the text box that appears
   - Copy and paste the message below:

```
Hello po! 👋 Salamat sa pag-message sa Ultiphoton Solar Power OPC. 

We're currently assisting other clients or baka out of office hours po, pero we want to help you right away! Paki-check po ang aming menu sa ibaba para sa mga common questions tulad ng prices, locations, at solar packages. 

Kung wala po sa menu ang tanong ninyo, please leave your message here at babalikan po namin kayo as soon as possible. Maraming salamat po sa pag-consider sa solar energy! ☀️
```

3. **Save**
   - Click **"Save"** or **"Done"**
   - You should see a confirmation message

✅ **Instant Reply is now LIVE!**

## Step 3: Add All 15 FAQs

### Setup Instructions:

1. **Enable Frequently Asked Questions**
   - In Messaging settings, find **"Frequently Asked Questions"** or **"FAQs"**
   - Toggle the switch to **ON** (it should turn blue)

2. **Add First FAQ**
   - Click **"Add FAQ"** or **"Add Question"** button
   - You'll see two fields:
     - **Question:** (What customers will ask)
     - **Answer:** (Your response)

3. **Copy-Paste Method**
   - For each FAQ below:
     - Copy the **Question** text
     - Paste it in the "Question" field
     - Copy the **Answer** text
     - Paste it in the "Answer" field
     - Click **"Save"** or **"Add"**

4. **Repeat for All 15 FAQs**
   - Follow the same process for each FAQ below
   - Take your time - there's no rush!

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Can't find "Automations" or "Messaging" settings

**Solution:**
1. Go to your Facebook Page
2. Click "Settings" (gear icon, top right)
3. In the left menu, look for:
   - "Inbox" → "Automations"
   - OR "Messaging" → "Automations"
   - OR "Messaging" → "Instant Replies"

### Problem: FAQ isn't showing up after I save it

**Solution:**
- Wait 5-10 minutes for it to sync
- Refresh your browser
- Try adding it again
- Make sure you clicked "Save" or "Done"

### Problem: Instant Reply isn't working

**Solution:**
- Make sure the toggle is ON (blue)
- Check that you have admin access to the page
- Wait 5-10 minutes for it to activate
- Test by sending a message to your page from another account

### Problem: Text is too long and won't fit

**Solution:**
- Facebook has character limits (usually 2000 characters per FAQ)
- If your answer is too long, try:
  - Shortening it
  - Breaking it into multiple FAQs
  - Using bullet points instead of paragraphs

### Problem: Emojis aren't showing up

**Solution:**
- Some browsers have issues with emojis
- Try copying from a different source
- Or manually type the emoji using your keyboard

### Problem: I need to edit an FAQ after saving

**Solution:**
- Go back to the FAQ settings
- Find the FAQ you want to edit
- Click "Edit" or the pencil icon
- Make your changes
- Click "Save"
