# Talesun Solar Panel Specifications

## Talesun 620W Bifacial (TM3G66M-620)
- **Model**: BIPRO TM3G66M 16BB (Silver Frame)
- **Power Output**: 620W (Range: 610-630W)
- **Technology**: Bifacial, TOPCon, N-type
- **Cell Type**: Monocrystalline, 132 cells
- **Dimensions**: 93.78 x 44.65 x 1.18 inches (2,382 x 1,134 x 30 mm)
- **Weight**: 74.29 lbs (33.7 kg)
- **Module Efficiency**: ~22.8%
- **Operating Voltage (Vmpp)**: 41.10V
- **Open-Circuit Voltage (Voc)**: 49.00V
- **Short-Circuit Current (Isc)**: ~15.8A
- **Operating Current (Impp)**: ~15.1A
- **Maximum String Voltage**: 1500V
- **Warranty**: 12 Years
- **Features**: Dual glass, bifacial backsheet, half-cut cell technology

## Key Benefits of Talesun Bifacial Panels
- **Bifacial Gain**: Up to 25% additional power generation from rear-side reflection
- **High Efficiency**: N-type TOPCon technology for superior performance
- **Durability**: Dual glass construction for enhanced protection
- **Temperature Coefficient**: Lower temperature coefficient means better performance in hot climates
- **Reliability**: 12-year product warranty

## Technical Notes
- Both panels use advanced N-type bifacial technology
- Half-cut cell design reduces internal resistance and improves reliability
- Silver aluminum frame provides excellent durability
- Suitable for both residential and commercial installations
- Maximum string voltage of 1500V allows for flexible system design
