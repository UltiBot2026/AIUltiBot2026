## ⚙️ How to Set Up Your Facebook Automated Responses

Follow these simple steps to implement the automated responses for your Ultiphoton Solar Power OPC Facebook page.

### Step 1: Access Meta Business Suite
1. Log in to Facebook and go to your **Ultiphoton Solar Power OPC** page.
2. On the left menu, click on **Meta Business Suite**.
3. In the Business Suite menu, click on **Inbox**.
4. At the top right of the Inbox, click the **Automations** icon (it looks like a little robot or atom symbol).

### Step 2: Set Up the Welcome Message (Instant Reply)
1. In the Automations menu, find and click on **Instant Reply**.
2. Toggle the switch to turn it **ON**.
3. Check the box for **Messenger** (and Instagram if you have it connected).
4. In the **Message** box, copy and paste the Welcome Message from the template document.
5. Click **Save Changes** at the bottom right.

### Step 3: Set Up the FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)
1. Go back to the main Automations menu.
2. Find and click on **Frequently Asked Questions**.
3. Toggle the switch to turn it **ON**.
4. Check the box for **Messenger**.
5. Click **+ Add another question**.
6. **Question Box:** Copy the question from the template (e.g., "Magkano po ang solar panels?").
7. **Message Box:** Copy the corresponding automated answer from the template.
8. **Add Media (Optional but Recommended):** 
   - For FAQ 6 (Accessories), you can attach the price list images you provided.
   - For FAQ 9 (Inverter Brands), you can attach the logos of Deye, Solis, GoodWe, SRNE, and Sigenergy.
9. **Add Button (Optional):** You can add a button like "Visit Website" or "Call Us" with your link/number.
10. Repeat steps 5-9 for all 15 FAQs.
11. Click **Save Changes** at the bottom right.

### 💡 Pro Tips for a "Genuine" Feel
- **Don't use all 15 FAQs in the main menu:** Facebook only shows a few questions initially. Put your top 4-5 most common questions (Pricing, Location, Free Inspection, Installation Time) in the main menu.
- **Use Keyword Triggers:** For the remaining questions, you can set up "Custom Keywords" in Automations. If someone types "COD" or "Installment", the system will automatically send the corresponding answer.
- **Always follow up:** Even with automated responses, try to have a human check the inbox at least twice a day to answer complex questions or follow up on the automated replies.
- **Update regularly:** If prices change or new stock arrives, make sure to update the automated responses immediately so customers always get accurate information.
